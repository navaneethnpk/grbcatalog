Parameter Info

Parameter Name Description
GRB Name GRB name in the 'GCN' format, i.e. GRB010513B is the second GRB on the 13th of May 2001.
Trigger Time UTC time at which the GRB triggered the satellite.
AstroSat Time This time corresponds to the trigger time of the transient calculated in the Astrosat Seconds. Time is desplayed in seconds.
RA Right ascension in J2000 coordinates.
Dec Declination in J2000 coordinates.
T90 Duration of the time interval during which 90% of the GRB photon counts are observed, starting at 5% and ending at 95%.
T90 Error Estimated 1σ error on the duration of the T90 interval.
Fluence The time integrated total energy per unit surface.
Fluence Error Estimated 1σ error on the fluence.
Orbit_Number The Orbit number in which the transient is observed in the CZTI data.
T90(CZTI) Duration of the time interval during which 90% of the fluence is observed, starting at 5% and ending at 95%.
Compton_Counts The number of events recorded when subjected to compton scattering criteria. It is depicted in counts per sec i.e. count rate.
Theta_X, Theta_Y The direction in which CZTI is pointing in the camera coordinates system. The theta X and theta Y are displayed in degree.
Theta The azimuthal angle of the instruent in camera coordinate system.
Phi The polar angle of the instruent in camera coordinate system.
AstroSat Detection Whether the transient is observed in CZTI or not. I is defined by flag value 0 for non detection and 1 for detection.
